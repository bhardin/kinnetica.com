  • Jekyll Plugin: Sitemap.xml Generator

    This plugin will traverse through all the pages and posts within a Jekyll site at the time the site is regenerated and create a sitemap.xml file within the _sites/ folder. The sitemap.xml file is deployed along with your site and submitted to search engines to ensure that all of your pages are being indexed correctly.
